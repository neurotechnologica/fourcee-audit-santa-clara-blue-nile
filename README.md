# Fourcee - AI Concierge Growth Engine

Fourcee is a conversion system for high-end jewelers that combines:

- a premium marketing site,
- an interactive "free audit" lead magnet,
- package selection and checkout,
- and a concierge intelligence portal simulation.

The core business model is simple: use a hyper-personalized free audit to show each showroom exactly how much revenue is being lost to missed calls, then convert qualified leads into monthly or annual concierge plans.

---

## What This Product Does

This project delivers a full top-of-funnel to conversion experience:

- **Landing experience** that positions Fourcee as a luxury AI concierge.
- **Free audit page** tailored to a specific business, designed to create urgency and trust.
- **Checkout flow** with monthly vs annual billing and setup-fee logic.
- **CRM/portal simulation** that demonstrates "live concierge operations" (active calls, transcripts, history, metrics).

It is built as a reusable template so each new client audit can be generated by injecting scraped + computed data.

---

## Business Model and Offer Structure

Fourcee is sold as a managed AI concierge deployment for jewelers:

- **Starter** - entry concierge coverage
- **Pro** - growth-focused operations + richer AI capabilities
- **Enterprise** - multi-store and deeper integrations
- **Diamond** - custom enterprise expansion tier

### Billing Logic

- All tiers have **monthly** and **annual** pricing.
- **Monthly plans include a one-time setup fee.**
- **Annual plans waive setup fees.**

The checkout and pricing cards are built to reinforce this value framing clearly and consistently.

---

## Free Audit Hook (Lead Magnet Strategy)

The free audit is the main lead acquisition mechanism.

### Why it converts

The audit is designed to trigger three powerful buyer responses:

1. **Personal relevance** - "This was made specifically for my showroom."
2. **Financial urgency** - quantified missed monthly revenue and lost opportunity.
3. **Low-friction next step** - immediate path to "go live in 2 hours."

### Audit conversion flow

1. Prospect lands on a personalized audit URL.
2. A confidential splash screen creates exclusivity and trust.
3. Report quantifies call volume, answer-rate gaps, and revenue leakage.
4. Visuals and breakdowns make losses concrete and emotionally compelling.
5. User tests concierge call behavior and sees proof elements.
6. CTA routes directly into package selection and checkout.

This "free value first" approach positions Fourcee as diagnostic + solution, not just software.

  - the personalized audit experience.

- `constants.tsx`
  - package definitions, pricing, feature lists, and commercial copy anchors.

- `types.ts`
  - shared types for views, package structure, checkout state.

## Notes for Operators

- Keep the audit premium, specific, and easy to scan.
- Never publish hardcoded metrics for a live client without replacing template data.
- Ensure monthly vs annual pricing and setup logic always match commercial policy.
- Revalidate all client-facing numbers when changing package or benchmark assumptions.
